Our Educational Campaign

Pancasila aims to bring Indonesia’s foundational values to life for people of all ages, especially young audiences. Through interactive workshops, engaging social media posts, and hands-on activities, the campaign introduces the five guiding principles of Pancasila, highlighting their role in promoting unity, tolerance, and justice in our diverse society. Public service announcements, creative challenges, and school programs will make these values relatable and accessible, inspiring you to apply them in your daily life. By participating, you’ll gain a deeper connection to Indonesia’s identity and see firsthand how Pancasila shapes our communities and future. Join us in celebrating and living these shared values!
